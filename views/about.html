Camp Decree

Camp Wasatch is designed to help participants get away form the world of phones, emails, and social media pressures. To help with this goal there are rules that must be followed.

  • No devices:

    • Leave your phones, tablets, mac books, and smart watches at home. This is your chance to be free from all technology.

  • No business jargon:

    • We want Camp to be about making real connections with people and nature. This means no work talk, no networking, and no job titles.

  • No real names:

    • This an opportunity lose yourself, so at Camp Wasatch we only use nicknames.

  • Meals:

    • All meals at Camp are provided for by world class catering. Let us know if you have any dietary restriciotns when you register.

  • Length:

    • Camp lasts for a total of 3 days, 2 nights.

  • Pack:

    • Be sure to bring a sleeping bag, clothes, toothbrush, sunglasses, sunscreen, swimming suit, hiking shoes. We will provide the lodging, the activities, the games, the training, and camp fire.
